Adjusted Body Weight (AdjBW)

--kg
BMI (kg/m²) = Weight (kg) / [Height (m)]²
BSA (m²) = √([Height (cm) × Weight (kg)] / 3600)
IBW (kg, Devine) = Men: 50kg + 0.9kg × (Height cm - 152) | Women: 45.5kg + 0.9kg × (Height cm - 152)
AdjBW (kg) = IBW + 0.4 × (Actual Weight - IBW)

Renal Function Calculator (CrCl)

For obese patients, use Adjusted Body Weight (AdjBW).

Creatinine Clearance (CrCl)

--ml/min
CrCl (ml/min) = [(140 - Age) × Body Weight (kg) × F] / Serum Creatinine (μmol/L)
F = 1.23 for males, 1.04 for females.
Based on the Cockcroft-Gault equation.
